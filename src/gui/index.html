Objective

How to Play

  • Players take turns placing their pieces on empty cells
  • Once placed, pieces cannot be moved or removed
  • A winning path must be continuous (connected neighbors)
  • Corner cells count as part of both adjacent edges

Winning

  • First player to complete their connection wins
  • The game cannot end in a draw (mathematical proof exists)
